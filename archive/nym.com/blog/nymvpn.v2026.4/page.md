Introducing new ad blocker for NymVPN on desktop apps, plus improvements to app accessibility

**Versions:** iOS v2.19.0 | macOS v2.19.0 | Android v2.10.0 | Linux v1.25.0 | Windows v1.25.0

**NymVPN v2026.4** is now live and introduces a new **ad blocker** for desktop apps.

Improvements have also been made to alternative login and account recovery options.

Apple Store and Google Play have been localized into **9 languages**, with many more to come.

## New ad blocking for NymVPN

![Image 4](https://nym.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fassets.nym.com%2FNym_VPN_ad_blocker_f5fbcb8f0e.png&w=3840&q=75)
**Blocking ads** is actually a VPN superpower when it comes to privacy: ads often function as spyware to track your behaviour across web sessions. NymVPN can now be a first line of defense. Simply toggle it on in **Settings** and get ad free.

NymVPN's ad blocker is now available for desktop apps on **macOS**, **Windows**, and **Linux**. Mobile versions are in development for upcoming releases, so hold tight!

## Improved alternative logins and account recovery

![Image 5](https://nym.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fassets.nym.com%2FNym_VPN_Introducing_social_login_option_94f63db586.png&w=3840&q=75)
In addition to logging into or creating an anonymous NymVPN account, NymVPN also allows you to use another personal account for the convenience of logging in or to safely recover your passphrase.

V2026.4 improves on the alternative account creation path for new users.

## NymVPN localizations

### App store localizations

Apple Store and Google Play have now been **localized in 9 languages** to increase NymVPN’s global reach.

*   French
*   German
*   Han Chinese (Simplified)
*   Hindi
*   Portuguese
*   Russian
*   Spanish
*   Turkish
*   Ukrainian

### Nym.com

[Nym.com](https://nym.com/) has now been localized in **Han Chinese (simplified)**! **Farsi** localization is coming shortly.
