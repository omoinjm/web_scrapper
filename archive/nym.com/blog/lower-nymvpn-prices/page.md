# A radically lower price for NymVPN: How we fight for an open internet for everyone

Making NymVPN accessible to people all over the world to fight censorship and build an open and private internet

Nym is rolling out a **new low price for NymVPN** which we hope will make the app significantly more accessible to people around the world.

As censorship and surveillance intensifies across the world, NymVPN is now functioning in some of the most restrictive environments. The app should also be accessible long-term to people living there.

When you connect to NymVPN, your internet activity becomes untraceable from hostile entities, and you can access whatever part of the internet which was blocked for you. Price shouldn’t be an obstacle to people climbing the walls around them, so we’ve decided to make our technology more accessible to all.

## New prices in effect

NymVPN prices have been lowered on both [nym.com](https://nym.com/) and in app stores. Plans start as low as **$2.39 USD / 2.69 € / £ 2.29 per month**. This is a **47% decrease on monthly plans** and up to **37% decrease on long-term plans**.

## More to come

This is just a first step in improving [NymVPN](https://nym.com/)’s global reach across all social layers. For Nym, everyone needs privacy protections because [surveillance affects everyone](https://nym.com/blog/nym-stands-against-censorship). So stay tuned, we’re just getting started.

And remember, if you or your loved ones are **living in a place with restricted internet access**, reach out to [NymVPN’s Customer Support](https://support.nym.com/hc/en-us/requests/new) and we’ll help you out.

## Were you a NymVPN Early Bird?

To make sure your support is recognized as promised, **[reach out to Customer Support](https://support.nym.com/hc/en-us/requests/new) with your account number** and we’ll top up your account with **an extra 6 months of subscription time**.
