Nym prioritizes security with an open-source approach and undergoes regular independent security audits by leading firms and cryptographers.

## Science

Nym is built on cutting-edge technologies developed by our co-founders and peer-reviewed in top-tier publications. Continuous innovation is driven by our advisory board and research partnerships.

[Science ### Papers and research Technologies powering Nym have been developed by our founders, employees, and advisors, and published in prominent peer-reviewed cryptography and computer science journals.](https://nym.com/trust-center/papers-and-research)

[Science ### Up-to-date cryptography Nym utilizes robust cryptographic primitives like Curve25519, AES, ChaChaPoly and BLAKE, as well as zero-knowledge proofs for enhanced security.](https://nym.com/trust-center/cryptography)

[Science ### Nym advisors Nym is guided by renowned professors, cryptographers, and engineers who have contributed to the design of modern web standards such as OpenSSL and TLS, as well as various ciphers and hash functions.](https://nym.com/about#team-members-133)

[Science ### Research partnerships Nym collaborates with leading universities and organizations to advance privacy, network security, cryptography, and beyond. Contact us for partnership opportunities!](https://nym.com/trust-center/research-partnerships)
