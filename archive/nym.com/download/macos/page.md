### FAQs: NymVPN for macOS

### How to use NymVPN on macOS?

To use NymVPN on macOS, subscribe to NymVPN, download and install the macOS app from nym.com, and log into the app with your recovery phrase.

### Why is macOS 13 or earlier not supported by NymVPN?

Apple stopped support for macOS Ventura 13 at the end of 2025. NymVPN now requires macOS 14 or above. You can [read more here](https://nym.com/blog/nymvpn-mac-ventura-13).

### What is a VPN in macOS?

With NymVPN on macOS, all the data coming to and from your macOS device will be protected against surveillance.
