### Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ) - NymVPN on iOS

### What is a mobile VPN?

A mobile VPN is a VPN service that you can use on your mobile devices like a smartphone. It is an important option to look for when choosing a VPN provider, since all the data on our phones is subject to surveillance and exploitation just as easily as that from our computers. Not all VPN providers include mobile versions. NymVPN has a mobile VPN for all your needed devices.

### Why do you need a mobile VPN?

Smartphones have become essential everyday tools for connecting to the web. This means that a lot of personal data is becoming available and vulnerable through our smartphones. A mobile VPN like NymVPN can help protect your data just as it would on your computer.

### What is a VPN in iOS?

With NymVPN on iOS, all the data coming to and from your iOS device will be protected against surveillance just like with a VPN on your computer.

### How to use NymVPN on iOS?

To use NymVPN on iOS, subscribe to NymVPN, download the app from the Apple App Store (or Apple TestFlight), and log into the app with your recovery phrase.
