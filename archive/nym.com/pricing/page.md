## NymVPN pricing: FAQs

### Can I try NymVPN for free?

Yes, NymVPN offers a 7-day free trial for new users. Users can cancel the trial before it ends exactly 7 days from activation of the account. When the free trial is complete, the subscription will automatically convert to the subscription plan selected at current pricing unless the customer cancels beforehand.

Activating a NymVPN free trial is only available with certain payment types and providers, and requires a payment method should the customer decide to keep using the product beyond the trial period. No charges are made to the card at the time of activating the free trial. Free trials cannot be activated using cryptocurrency payments.

### What payment methods can I use to buy NymVPN?

You can pay for NymVPN using:

*   Cryptocurrencies: Bitcoin (BTC), ADA, Lightning Network (BTC-LN), Ethereum (ETH) tokens, Binance (BNB) tokens, Liquid Bitcoin (L-BTC), Liquid Tether (L-USDT), Litecoin (LTC), Litecoin MWEB (LTC-MWEB), Monero (XMR), $NYM, Tether (USDT on TRON), Zcash (ZEC)
*   Credit/debit cards: All major cards accepted via Stripe
*   In-app payments through Apple Pay and Google Pay
*   [GNU Taler](https://nym.com/blog/taler) a privacy-focused digital cash service
*   Cash

Additional payment methods are added regularly. [Tell us](https://support.nym.com/hc/en-us/requests/new) which private payments method you would like us to add next!

### Does my NymVPN subscription auto-renew?

All plans will auto-renew unless you cancel prior to the renewal date indicated on their plan and their account.

### Does NymVPN offer refunds?

Yes, NymVPN can offer pro-rata refunds if you are unsatisfied.

### How long does it take to receive my refund?

Refund processing times vary by payment method. For card payments via Stripe, refunds typically take 5-10 business days. $NYM token refunds can be processed instantly, but final settlement depends on blockchain network congestion.

### How private are NymVPN payments?

With the [zk-nyms' anonymous access credentials](https://nym.com/zk-nyms), your payment data cannot be linked to your online activity with NymVPN.

Your payments may be visible in your own financial history or on public blockchains. For card payments, our payment processor Stripe stores only the data necessary to process the transaction. Please read our [Privacy Policy](https://nym.com/vpn-privacy-statement) for more information on data processing with NymVPN.

### In what currencies can I buy a NymVPN subscription?

NymVPN subscriptions are available in EUR (€), USD ($), GBP (£), and CHF, with many cryptocurrency payments also supported. Available currencies depend on your location and chosen payment method.

### What are NymVPN’s future payment options?

We regularly add new payment methods based on user feedback. New payment methods are prioritized by user demand.

### What happens if my NymVPN payment fails?

If your payment fails, please verify your payment details and try again. If the issue persists, [contact Customer Support](https://support.nym.com/hc/en-us/requests/new) for assistance.

### What devices and operating systems does NymVPN support?

NymVPN aims to support a wide range of devices and operating systems, including but not limited to Android, iOS, (GNU/)Linux, macOS, and Windows. Check the list below to see which operating systems are supported.

### Windows
