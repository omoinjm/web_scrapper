# Private Together

At Nym, we believe that privacy is a fundamental right for everyone. Our mission is to create technology that empowers individuals to take control of their digital lives, to overcome barriers to information, and to foster a global community dedicated to making it possible.

### Nym's principles

![Image 3](https://nym.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fassets.nym.com%2FDecentralized_4cb0dfe62f_0db56b4b7a.svg&w=256&q=75)

### Decentralized

No central point of failure. A network designed to protect everyone's data and activities from surveillance.

![Image 4](https://nym.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fassets.nym.com%2FEnjoy_f75a0bb6f8_f5e46b377c.svg&w=256&q=75)

### Trustless

Private by design. Nym's zero-knowledge network takes trust out of the equation when it comes to who handles your data.

![Image 5](https://nym.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fassets.nym.com%2FStake_12542dd031_ef1df96fa8.svg&w=256&q=75)

### Tokenized

Incentivized network performance. Powered by the NYM utility token to ensure the economic sustainability of operators who make possible the most anonymous network available.

![Image 6](https://nym.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fassets.nym.com%2FOpen_Source_ec51d50057_f40be7444f.svg&w=256&q=75)

### Open Source

Publicly auditable code. All Nym software can be audited by anyone, anytime. Peer validation ensures that it is secure and of the highest quality. And no backdoors!

![Image 7](https://nym.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fassets.nym.com%2FAccess_blocked_Content_be2d3ea197_1_15085a3ef1_a8b8fd9d41.svg&w=256&q=75)

### Permissionless

Anyone can join and run Nym software. Spin up a Nym node yourself and contribute to Nym's mission of providing privacy to all.

![Image 8](https://nym.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fassets.nym.com%2FWorld_class_team_36aee8396f_7d3fd4b645.svg&w=256&q=75)

### Collective

A private network built together. The Nym network is possible through a global community of operators, privacy enthusiasts, and activists.

### Prof. Daniel J. Bernstein

Mathematician, cryptologist, computer scientist

Daniel is renowned for his seminal contributions to cryptography. His work focuses on designing cryptographic systems to proactively reduce risks. ### NYM Roadmap

### 2018–2020: Cypherpunks dream of mixnets

*   Set up the foundations of the Nym mixnet in 2018
*   Onboarded the first mix nodes and validators after the launch at 2019 Chaos Communication Congress
*   Raised a 2.5M CHF pre-seed round led by Binance Labs
*   Published the Nym Litepaper
*   Built the mixnet in Rust for better performance
*   Opened the “Nymph” tokenized testnet to public
*   Launched the Nym Wallet and Explorers

### 2021: Year of the mainnet

*   Published the Nym whitepaper
*   Completed the "Finney" and "Milhon" testnets
*   Closed a 6M CHF seed round led by Polychain
*   Closed a 13M CHF funding round led by a16z (Andreessen Horowitz)
*   Launched the NYX chain
*   Held the token generation event
*   Went live with the Nym tokenized mainnet

### 2022: Incentivizing privacy

#### NYM token and tooling

*   Did the NYM token public sale on CoinList
*   Introduced mix nodes, staking, token liquidity, and custody into the Nym ecosystem
*   Published the Nym tokenomics paper("Reward sharing for mixnets")
*   Released the new Nym Wallet
*   Released the NymConnect app with SOCKS5 proxy to the mixnet
*   Added Telegram, Electrum Wallet, Matrix, and Keybase integrations in NymConnect

#### Community foundations

*   Co-founded the Universal Privacy Alliance with Manta, Secret, Orchid, ECC, Status, Oasis, Aztec, Aleo, MobileCoin, Anoma, and Railgun
*   Launched the first "Nym Shipyard Academy" with 6k participants
*   Allocated the first round of Shipyard grants
*   Launched the "Nym Innovation Fund"

### 2023: Becoming a global community

#### Nym tooling

*   Released the Rust and TypeScript Nym SDKs
*   Announced the launch of zk-nyms at SXSW
*   Launched mixFetch, a mixnet-friendly version of fetch
*   Published the NymVPN Litepaper
*   Presented NymVPN at the Chaos Communication Congress
*   Launched nymvpn.com

#### The Nym Network

*   Ecosystem in Latin America established, growing to 30+ nodes in the region
*   Launched the "Nymja" ambassador program
*   Integrated the Nym mixnet with Blockstream green, Monero, Alephium wallets
*   Partnered with Chainsafe for shielded Ethereum consensus clients, Side protocol, Aztec, Near, and Woleet
*   Opened the London and Paris offices
*   Organized the first community censorship tests
*   Launched the Delegations Program (30M NYM tokens) to support decentralization and Nym node operator growth

### 2024: Launching NymVPN & growing the community

#### Launching NymVPN

*   Launched NymVPN in alpha and beta for free public testing
*   Launched the NymVPN Help Center and Trust Center
*   Added a WireGuard-based "Fast Mode" for everyday privacy
*   Conducted the first security audit of the NymVPN apps (Cure53)

#### Growing the Nym Network & Community

*   Launched the "Nym Squad League"
*   Completed “Project Smoosh”, enableing operators to run both mixnodes and gateways
*   Introduced a new rewards scheme for Nym nodes with gateway rewarding
*   Scaled up the "Nym Delegations Program" with rolling registrations, community-built applicant management system, and an increased budget of 40M NYMs
*   Built new operators tooling

### 2025: Scaling NymVPN and launching new products

#### New NymVPN features

*   Launch a new unified Nym brand and nym.com website
*   Turn on zk-nyms in mainnet for all NymVPN users
*   Add a killswitch mode for the NymVPN apps
*   Add split tunneling, ad blocker, post-quantum security and multiple other privacy and delight features to NymVPN

#### Improving the Nym network

*   Scale NymVPN to 100k users
*   Scale to 100+ entry and exit gateways
*   Extend mixnet support to blockchains
*   Go beyond NymVPN and start B2B deals with privacy tech and crypto companies
