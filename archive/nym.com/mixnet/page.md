## Multi-layer encryption

Multiple layers of encryption like an onion protect your data on a decentralized network.

![Image 5: Indistinguishable-packets.svg](https://nym.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fassets.nym.com%2FIndistinguishable_packets_light_799f99f358.svg&w=1200&q=80)

## Indistinguishable packets

With Sphinx encryption, data packets are of identical size and form, making it impossible for surveillance to identify you based on the content of your traffic.

![Image 6: Cover-Traffic.svg](https://nym.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fassets.nym.com%2FCover_traffic_light_1907b46fe9.svg&w=1200&q=80)

## Cover traffic

Empty or decoy data packets sent through the network with your actual data to obscure real traffic patterns.

![Image 7: Data-mixing.svg](https://nym.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fassets.nym.com%2FData_mixing_light_e85d4de41c.svg&w=1200&q=80)

## Data mixing

Mix nodes further obscure the link between sender and receiver in the network by shuffling the packets of users together and delaying their routing to other nodes.

### 5-hop mixnet

Data sent via the mixnet to any web application travels 5-hops before arriving at its destination. Between an Entry Node and an Exit node, three layers of mix nodes guarantee metadata obfuscation.

![Image 9: Advanced_Privacy_In_One_App_635bedcdfc.svg](https://nym.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fassets.nym.com%2FAdvanced_Privacy_In_One_App_635bedcdfc_f08a4866af.svg&w=256&q=80)

### Multi-layer encryption

At each subsequent hop, the data packets are encrypted so each node only has information about the packet's origin and destination but not the payload (data) itself.

![Image 10: Unlinkable_48bffc7e3a.svg](https://nym.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fassets.nym.com%2FUnlinkable_48bffc7e3a_4d8ee4314d.svg&w=256&q=80)

When you connect to NyMVPN, the Entry Gateway performs the check for a zero-knowledge network access credential, making users un-linkable to their internet usage.

### Nym's Noise Generating Mixnet: FAQs

### How can I use the Nym NGM?

**Individuals:**

You can simply get the NymVPN app to start benefiting from the secure routing offered by the Nym network. The app allows people to choose between two network modes:

*   **Fast Mode** (decentralized 2-hop), optimized for browsing and streaming
*   **Anonymous Mode** (a 5-hop NGM) optimized for anonymity and best for messaging, crypto transactions, email, and other activities that can tolerate high latency.

**Enterprise:**

For companies seeking to offer superior security and integrity to their customers, the Nym network can be used as a general purpose secure routing network. Nym can be integrated directly with applications and services to secure the integrity of users and systems by providing secure routing for any app, blockchain, or service. Integrations currently in progress include z-cash light clients to protect crypto transactions and Ethereum validators to secure the integrity of consensus and protect against MEV attacks. Contact us at: [contact@nym.com](https://nym.com/contact@nym.com).

### Why is the Nym network decentralized?

Decentralization of the mixnet improves its fundamental privacy properties like ensuring that users are not directly connected to their online interactions.

Decentralization ensures that the full route of a user’s traffic can never be known by any single entity handling user data, and makes external surveillance very difficult to achieve.

Decentralization requires a minimum of 2 intermediary servers (or hops) between a user and the web. The large majority of VPNs are centralized. With NymVPN, there is no single entity on the network, not even Nym, where your full traffic is logable. Nym is a zero-knowledge network by design.

### What are the benefits of a Noise Generating Mixnet (NGM)?

A Noise Generating Mixnet (NGM) is a new technology for secure internet traffic routing, developed specifically for the growing challenges posed by AI and malicious government surveillance programs. A NGM protects not just the content of messages but also the metadata and patterns of communication across the internet.

A NGM performs best in class (over VPNs and Tor) against:

*   Traffic analysis
*   End-to-end correlation attacks
*   Website fingerprinting
*   Protecting your traffic from a global passive adversary able to watch the entire network.

It does this by introducing “noise” into the network to protect users’ metadata and obscure their patterns of communication. On Nym’s NGM, noise includes uniform packet sizes, cover traffic, packet mixing, and timing obfuscation. Network noise makes it so everyone’s traffic cannot be analyzed and used by fraudsters, hackers, and malicious actors to perform a variety of attacks.

### What is the speed and latency of the Nym NGM?

The speed of the Nym network depends on your choice of routing. Through NymVPN, the normal 2-hop (WireGuard) mode has speeds up to 100 Mbps and latency around 50 ms depending on your chosen gateways. This is ideal for things like browsing and streaming.

For maximal anonymity protections , the Anonymous Mode will involve more latency but with advanced protections against surveillance. It is thus recommended for highly sensitive traffic which can tolerate high latency like messaging, crypto transactions, and email.
